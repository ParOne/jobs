### 1. Edit Instructional Training Content
- Take raw footage from golf instructors and coaches
- Edit clean, professional **landscape videos** for in-product training

- Maintain consistent pacing, tone, and visual style across content

### 2. Create Marketing and Product Media
- Produce trailers, promos, and short-form clips for marketing
- Cut product explainers and feature walkthroughs
- Adapt long-form content into short, reusable assets

### 3. Prepare Content for Multiple Use Cases
- Deliver edits optimized for web, mobile, and CTV
- Export multiple aspect ratios and durations as needed
- Organize and manage project files cleanly for reuse and iteration

### 4. Collaborate Across Teams
- Work with instructors to refine delivery and structure
- Coordinate with product and marketing on content needs
- Iterate quickly based on feedback and product changes
