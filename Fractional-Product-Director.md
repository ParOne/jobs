## About the Company

ParOne builds software products for sports and media.

The client product suite includes:
- **Flights** — scoped, time-boxed shoppable / interactive product integrations inside of SIMID ads.
- **FAST Channel CMS** — content management for Free Ad-Supported Streaming TV channels for owned and licensed content
- **Shoppable Ads** — embedded commerce within live and on-demand FAST content
- **Custom Video Player** — Proprietary video player with native commerce capabilities

We're a small, remote team that values clear thinking, fast execution, and direct client communication. This role sits at the intersection of product strategy, client services, and technical scoping.

## What You'll Do
 
### 1. Define Scope for Digital Video Ad Campaigns
- Work directly with clients to understand their commerce and content goals
- Translate business requirements into well-defined product scope for each Flight
- Determine what features should be built, deferred, or handled via configuration
- Write clear, concise scope documents that engineering can act on immediately
- Own the scope review and sign-off process with clients before build begins
 
### 2. Own Product Requirements Across client
- Define and maintain requirements for the FAST Channel CMS, Shopping on FAST, and Custom Video Player
- Identify gaps between current capabilities and client or market needs
- Prioritize the backlog in partnership with engineering leadership
- Write product specs with enough detail to prevent scope creep and rework
 
### 3. Lead Client Discovery & Requirements Gathering
- Run discovery sessions with client
- Ask the right questions to surface unstated requirements and edge cases
- Build alignment between what clients want and what we can realistically deliver
- Serve as the primary product point of contact for client-facing conversations
 
### 4. Drive Fast, Iterative Delivery
- Help maintain a high-velocity delivery culture — scope tightly, ship quickly, iterate
- Flag scope risk early and propose tradeoffs before they become blockers
- Adapt requirements quickly when client or market conditions shift
